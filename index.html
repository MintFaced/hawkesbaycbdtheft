Methodology & Data Sources
Crime rates sourced from crimestats.co.nz and safesuburbs.co.nz, which aggregate official NZ Police monthly data releases from policedata.nz (Recorded Crime Victims Statistics, RCVS). Data period: 12 months to October 2025. Area units: Napier Central (meshblock 214300), Hastings Central (210500), Havelock North-Central (211600), Taradale Central (213700).

Thefts per business estimates are derived by applying the published theft percentage to total incident counts implied by per-1,000 rates, divided by CBD business membership counts. Napier: ~450 businesses (Napier City Business Inc.); Hastings: 480+ businesses (Hastings City Business Association); Havelock North: ~350 businesses (Havelock North Business Association); Taradale: ~200 businesses (Taradale Business Association).

Important caveat: Per-1,000-resident rates are inflated in CBDs due to very small resident populations relative to daytime visitor/worker footfall. The "thefts per business" metric is a more operationally meaningful comparator for business associations. For exact incident counts, download the raw monthly CSV from policedata.nz Victimisations report, filter by Eastern District > Hawke's Bay > area unit > Theft offence category.
